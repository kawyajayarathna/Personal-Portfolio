Fluent-Me

Spoken English Learning App

Project Overview

Fluent-Me is a spoken English learning application designed to help users improve their English speaking skills through interactive lessons, quizzes, and real-time feedback. The app features a user-friendly interface, personalized learning paths, and a variety of engaging content to enhance the learning experience. FluentMe - A mobile app that allows non-native English speakers to improve their spoken fluency, confidence, and pronunciation by engaging in daily challenge conversations,  simulation conversations, and positive feedback.
